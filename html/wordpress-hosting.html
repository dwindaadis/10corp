WordPress-Optimized

Not Just Regular Hosting for WordPress

Our WordPress Hosting is purpose-built on cloud infrastructure fine-tuned for databases, CDN delivery, and WordPress-specific caching — delivering measurably faster load times than generic shared hosting.

Pre-installed WordPress

WordPress comes pre-installed and configured. Log in and start creating content immediately — no setup required.

Integrated Caching & CDN

LiteSpeed Cache with built-in CDN delivers your content from global edge nodes, cutting load times dramatically for international visitors.

Auto Core Updates

We automatically update your WordPress core to the latest stable version, keeping you protected from known vulnerabilities without any manual effort.

Data Mirroring

Your data is mirrored across multiple nodes in real-time. If one node goes down, another instantly takes over — your site never skips a beat.

WP Malware Scanning

Daily automated malware scans with instant alerts and one-click cleanup — powered by SiteLock's WordPress-specific threat database.

Easy Scalability

Traffic spike? Scale resources up instantly from your dashboard. No downtime, no migration — your WordPress site grows with your audience.
