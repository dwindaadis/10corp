Our Mission

Empowering Every Business to Thrive Online

At Hostika, we believe that every idea deserves a reliable home on the internet. Founded in 2010 by a team of engineers and entrepreneurs, we set out to make enterprise-grade hosting accessible to everyone — from solo bloggers to Fortune 500 companies.

Today we operate 8 data centers across 4 continents, serving customers in over 120 countries. Our commitment to uptime, speed, and support has never wavered — because your success is our success.

How We Got Here

Our Journey

2010

The Beginning

Founded in a small office in San Francisco, Hostika launched with shared hosting for 50 beta customers — all friends and family.

2013

VPS & Dedicated Servers

Launched VPS and dedicated server offerings after reaching 10,000 customers. Opened our first data center in Dallas, TX.

2017

Going Global

Expanded to Europe and Asia with new data centers in Frankfurt and Singapore. Hit the 100,000 customer milestone.

2024

500K Customers & Beyond

Crossed the half-million customer mark with 8 data centers and a 24/7 expert support team of 300+ engineers worldwide.
