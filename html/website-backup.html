Why Backup Your Website?

Your Site Can Be Gone in Seconds

Hackers, bad updates, accidental deletions — it only takes one mistake. CodeGuard ensures you can restore everything, instantly.

Hacker Attacks

Malware and ransomware attacks can wipe your site completely. CodeGuard keeps clean, off-site copies so you can restore within minutes.

Bad Plugin Updates

A single faulty WordPress plugin update can break your entire site. Roll back to the pre-update backup in one click.

Accidental Deletions

Deleted an important page or file by mistake? Recover exactly what was there, down to the version you need.

Instant Change Monitoring

CodeGuard automatically detects any change to your website files and alerts you immediately — so you stay in control at all times.

Off-Site Cloud Storage

Your backups are stored completely off your hosting server — so even if your host goes down, your backup is safe and accessible.

Automatic Daily Backups

No manual effort required. CodeGuard runs silently every night, backing up files and databases without touching your site's performance.
