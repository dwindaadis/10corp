EFFECTIVE DATE: JANUARY 8, 2024. THIS PRIVACY POLICY EXPLAINS HOW 10CORP COLLECTS, USES, AND PROTECTS YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION.

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") applies to 10Corp Web Services LLC ("10Corp," "we," "us," "our") and all services provided through our Hostika brand and affiliated platforms. By using our services or visiting our websites, you consent to the data practices described in this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly, including: full name and contact details (email, phone, address); billing and payment information (processed via PCI-compliant payment processors — we do not store full card numbers); account login credentials; domain registrant information required by ICANN; communications you send to our support team; and content you upload to our platforms.

B. Information Collected Automatically

When you use our services, we automatically collect: IP address and approximate geographic location; browser type, version, and device information; pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs; server log data and error reports; and cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 6).

C. Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from payment processors, analytics providers, identity verification services, and domain registries as necessary to deliver our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to: provide, operate, and improve our services; process payments and manage your account; send transactional emails (receipts, renewal notices, support responses); send marketing communications where you have opted in; comply with ICANN requirements; detect and prevent fraud and security incidents; respond to legal requests; and conduct analytics to improve our experience.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process personal data under: Contract — processing necessary to deliver purchased services; Legal Obligation — ICANN WHOIS requirements and applicable law; Legitimate Interests — fraud prevention and security; Consent — marketing communications and non-essential cookies.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with: Service Providers (payment processors, hosting infrastructure, email delivery, analytics) strictly as needed to operate our services; Domain Registries as required by ICANN (WHOIS data); Legal Authorities when required by valid legal process; and in Business Transfers with prior notice.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Account data is retained for a minimum of five (5) years after account closure for legal and compliance purposes. ICANN requires domain registration data to be retained for two (2) years after domain expiration.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our websites use cookies to maintain session state, remember preferences, analyze traffic via tools such as Google Analytics, and deliver targeted advertising where applicable. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. We honor Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals where technically feasible.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption for data in transit, encrypted storage for sensitive data at rest, access controls and role-based permissions, and regular security audits. We will notify you of any data breach affecting your personal information as required by applicable law.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; correct inaccurate data; request deletion of your data; object to or restrict processing; port your data to another provider; and withdraw consent at any time. Contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within thirty (30) days.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell; to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell personal information); to request deletion of personal information; and to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights. See our Do Not Sell My Personal Information page or contact [email protected].

10. International Data Transfers

10Corp is based in the United States. For EEA users, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission for international transfers.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, contact us at [email protected] and we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of material changes via email or website notice at least thirty (30) days before the changes take effect.
