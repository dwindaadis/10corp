Email Marketing Services

10Corp Web Services LLC • Effective: Monday, January 8, 2024

PLEASE READ THIS AGREEMENT CAREFULLY. BY USING 10CORP'S EMAIL MARKETING SERVICES, YOU AGREE TO BE BOUND BY ALL TERMS AND CONDITIONS SET FORTH HEREIN.

This Email Marketing Services Agreement ("Agreement") governs your use of 10Corp Web Services LLC's ("10Corp," "we," "us," or "our") email marketing services, including campaign creation, list management, analytics, automation, and related tools provided through the Hostika platform or directly via 10Corp's marketing services division. By accessing or using these services, you agree to this Agreement, our Universal Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Anti-SPAM Policy.

COMPLIANCE NOTICE: Email marketing is governed by multiple federal, state, and international laws. You represent and warrant that all campaigns comply with the U.S. CAN-SPAM Act of 2003, EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL), and all other applicable laws and regulations in the jurisdictions where your recipients are located.

1. Description of Services

10Corp's Email Marketing Services may include any or all of the following, subject to your service plan:

  • Email campaign design, template creation, and scheduling;
  • Subscriber list management, segmentation, and tagging;
  • Automated email sequences, drip campaigns, and triggered messages;
  • A/B testing, subject line optimization, and send-time optimization;
  • Delivery analytics including open rates, click-through rates, bounce rates, and unsubscribe tracking;
  • Bounce management and automatic unsubscribe processing;
  • DKIM, SPF, and DMARC email authentication configuration;
  • Dedicated IP address management (where applicable at higher plan tiers);
  • Integrations with third-party CRM, e-commerce, and marketing automation platforms.

2. Acceptable Use Policy

A. Consent Requirements

You must only send email campaigns to recipients who have given prior, explicit consent to receive commercial email from you specifically. This means:

  • All email addresses on your subscriber lists must have clearly opted in to receive communications from you;
  • Double opt-in (confirmed opt-in) is strongly recommended and may be required for certain sending volumes or destination jurisdictions;
  • You must maintain auditable records of how and when each subscriber provided consent;
  • Purchased, rented, scraped, harvested, or otherwise third-party-acquired email lists are strictly prohibited.

B. Prohibited Content

You agree not to send email campaigns that contain, promote, or facilitate:

  • Unsolicited commercial email ("spam") or any content violating our Anti-SPAM Policy;
  • Pornographic, obscene, or sexually explicit material;
  • Content promoting violence, hatred, discrimination, or illegal activity;
  • Phishing, fraud, deception, or impersonation of any person or organization;
  • Malware, viruses, undisclosed tracking pixels, or any other harmful code;
  • Content that infringes any copyright, trademark, or other intellectual property rights;
  • Pharmaceutical products, controlled substances, or regulated goods without appropriate legal authorization;
  • Get-rich-quick schemes, multi-level marketing with false income claims, or pyramid schemes;
  • Any content that violates CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL, or other applicable email marketing regulations.

C. Required Email Elements

Every commercial email campaign sent through our platform must include:

  • Your full legal name or business name and a physical mailing address;
  • A clear, functional, one-click unsubscribe mechanism that remains active for at least thirty (30) days after sending and is honored within ten (10) business days;
  • Accurate "From," "Reply-To," and subject line information that is not deceptive or misleading;
  • Clear identification that the message is an advertisement where required by applicable law.

3. Sending Limits and Deliverability

A. Volume Limits

Sending volumes are subject to the limits of your current service plan as displayed in your account dashboard. Exceeding plan limits without prior arrangement may result in temporary suspension of sending privileges. 10Corp reserves the right to implement daily, weekly, or monthly sending limits to protect the deliverability reputation of all platform customers.

B. Bounce and Complaint Thresholds

You agree to maintain a hard bounce rate below 2% and a spam complaint rate below 0.1% of total emails sent within any rolling 30-day period. 10Corp monitors these metrics continuously. Exceeding these thresholds may result in:

  • Automatic suspension of your sending account pending investigation and review;
  • A mandatory requirement to conduct list hygiene and re-permission campaigns before sending resumes;
  • Permanent termination of Email Marketing Services at 10Corp's sole discretion for repeat or severe violations.

C. Deliverability Disclaimer

While 10Corp employs industry-standard practices to maximize email deliverability, we do not guarantee that any email will reach a recipient's inbox. Deliverability is affected by many factors outside our control, including recipient server configurations, spam filter rules, sender reputation, and recipient engagement history. 10Corp shall not be liable for any loss of revenue or opportunity resulting from emails that are filtered, blocked, or not delivered.

4. List Management and Data

A. Subscriber Data Ownership

You retain full ownership of your subscriber lists and all associated contact data stored on our platform. 10Corp will not sell, rent, or share your subscriber data with any third party, except as required by law or as described in our Privacy Policy.

B. Data Retention and Export

Subscriber data stored on 10Corp's platform will be retained for the duration of your active service agreement. Upon termination or cancellation of services, you will have thirty (30) days to export your subscriber data using the tools provided in your account. After this period, subscriber data will be permanently deleted from our systems and cannot be recovered.

C. International Data Compliance

If you send email campaigns to recipients in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, Canada, or other jurisdictions with specific data protection laws, you are solely responsible for ensuring your data collection, storage, and processing practices comply with all applicable regulations. This includes obtaining a lawful basis for processing personal data under GDPR, and providing required disclosures under CASL. 10Corp acts as a data processor on your behalf, and by using our services, you accept responsibility as the data controller for all subscriber data.

5. Fees and Payment

Email Marketing Services are offered under subscription plans based on subscriber count, monthly email volume, or a combination of both. Fees are charged in advance on a monthly or annual basis and are displayed in your account before each billing cycle. All fees are non-refundable except as provided in our Refund Policy. Overage charges may apply for usage exceeding plan limits. 10Corp reserves the right to modify pricing with at least thirty (30) days' advance notice.

6. Intellectual Property

You retain all ownership rights in the content of your email campaigns, including text, images, and branding. You grant 10Corp a limited, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to store, transmit, and display your campaign content solely as necessary to provide the Email Marketing Services. 10Corp's platform, templates, tools, algorithms, and infrastructure remain the exclusive intellectual property of 10Corp Web Services LLC.

7. Monitoring and Enforcement

10Corp reserves the right, but not the obligation, to monitor email campaigns for compliance with this Agreement and applicable law. We may suspend delivery of any campaign that we reasonably believe violates this Agreement, require modification or deletion of non-compliant content, terminate your access to Email Marketing Services for repeated or severe violations, and report suspected illegal activity to appropriate law enforcement or regulatory authorities.

This Agreement begins on the date you first access the Email Marketing Services and continues until terminated. Upon termination, your access to the platform will be disabled and you will have thirty (30) days to export your data before permanent deletion.
