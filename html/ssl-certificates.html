Why SSL?

Your Website Needs HTTPS

SSL is no longer optional — browsers flag HTTP sites as "Not Secure" and Google ranks HTTPS sites higher.

Encrypt All Data

SSL encrypts data between your server and visitors, protecting passwords, credit card info, and personal data from interception.

Boost SEO Rankings

Google confirmed HTTPS as a ranking signal. Secure your site with SSL and gain a competitive edge in search results.

Build Visitor Trust

The padlock icon and green address bar tell visitors your site is verified and safe, increasing conversions and reducing bounce rate.

Enable Online Payments

PCI-DSS compliance requires SSL for any website that accepts card payments. No SSL = no payments gateway approval.

Prevent "Not Secure" Warning

Chrome and Firefox display a red "Not Secure" warning on HTTP sites. An SSL certificate eliminates this and keeps visitors confident.

Financial Warranty

Sectigo SSL certificates come with a warranty of up to $500,000 to cover damages in the unlikely event of a mis-issuance.
