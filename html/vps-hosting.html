Why VPS?

The Sweet Spot Between Shared & Dedicated

VPS gives you dedicated resources, full root access, and complete isolation from other users — all at a price that scales with your business, not against it.

Complete Root Access

Install any OS, any software, any configuration. Your VPS, your rules. Linux (CentOS, Ubuntu, Debian, Fedora) or Windows available.

Guaranteed Resources

Your CPU, RAM, bandwidth, and storage are dedicated to you alone. No noisy neighbours affecting your performance.

Instant Scalability

Add RAM ($1/GB/mo), CPU cores ($2/core/mo), or storage in minutes from your dashboard. Scale up for traffic spikes, scale down when quiet.

Anti-DDoS Protection

Enterprise-grade DDoS mitigation protects your VPS from volumetric attacks, with firewall-level filtering at the network edge.
